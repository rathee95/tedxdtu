Story of Team Defianz Racing

DTU's Formula Student Team achieves remarkable feats

February 25, 2016 TEDxDTU

Abiding by its role as a platform for remarkable stories, the 'Xpress Your Story' campaign presents to you the story of Team Defianz Racing of Delhi Technological University, achieving the latest feather in their cap. "Team Defianz Racing, the Formula Student team of Delhi Technological University, was established in 2003 and since then, it has had a tremendous journey, defying odds at each stage and overcoming challenges to accomplish the brand name and regard it holds in the Indian Formula Student world today. Being the first team in Asia, it certainly was an uphill task, unimaginable to the majority, to conceptualize such a car and design and build it with the limited resources available at its disposal. In these 13 years, we have assembled 11 different cars, by capitalizing and improvising on each preceding one. The team dedicated nearly a year, beginning from February 2015, to develop their most technically complex car till date. After participating in Formula Student UK competitions over the years, we realized that this was the year when we had to test our limits, exceed expectations and build something which will supersede others at different levels. After deciding to take ample bold decisions for each system, from shifting to smaller wheels to buying new sophisticated parts and incorporating them perfectly, we worked hard to achieve each goal set at the beginning of the year. There were technical, managerial and financial challenges but nothing hampered our spirit. After spending 3 dedicated months of designing, we fought all odds to defy any kind of failure and fabricated what we call as the benchmark car in the team’s history. Our ambition made us the first Indian team to incorporate a full Aerodynamic package (front & rear wings and diffuser) in our car, manufactured from Carbon Fiber. While many others rely on external sources to fabricate their CF products, our team acquired the skills to fabricate each CF part in-house, a feat acknowledged by Formula LGB members at the recently concluded Formula Student India event, held at the Buddh International Circuit. The workshop became our second home, where the members would spend countless hours assembling each part, troubleshooting any problem and brainstorming for new innovative ideas that would help engineer a better car. Our idea of enjoying vacations is perfecting the car and running it around the college campus. The emotions involved and attached to the team for our members are so strong that we spent an entire week camping at a testing site, away from all comforts, and tested our car to ensure reliability and validation of designs. All our efforts came to light and showed a positive effect at Formula Student India. The team fought hard for the title, worked overnight to clear the scrutiny and gained an experience which we will cherish and remember for a long time. Apart from being technically sound, we proved our business acumen at the event by winning the First Runner Up trophy in the Business Plan event. Even after the competition, instead of relaxing, the team displayed its car at the biggest automobile event in the country, the Auto Expo ’16 at Greater Noida. As we conclude all Indian events, we will now be preparing for the upcoming Formula Student UK event in July ’16. We aim to outdo ourselves and earn accolades for our hard work and vision that went in the whole year."

About TEDx, x = independently organized event
In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TEDTalks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized(subject to certain rules and regulations).
