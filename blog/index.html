Mr. Anurag Batra

TEDxDTU presents our first speaker.

March 25, 2016 TEDxDTU
It is said that excellence thrives in the persona of a person not by chance but by a constant will to succeed in life.

Well Mr. Batra is no exception to this.Presenting to you our first speaker Mr. Anurag Batra, a visionary entrepreneur who has successfully donned multiple hats in his career- editor, writer, angel investor, event organizer and ofcourse that of a businessman. An alumnus of India's premier B-school, MDI, Gurgaon, Mr. Batra is the recipient of his Alma mater's "Most distinguished Alumni of the decade" award. Mr. Batra was a founding member of exchange4media that grew into the largest privately owned company in India. Mr.Batra acquired the leading business magazine, BW | Businessworld, of which he had been an ardent reader for nearly 25 years. He currently serves as the Chairman of GBN Media. Mr. Batra believes that in order to succeed , one must never take his eye off one's competitors. ‬ ‪

About TEDx, x = independently organized event
In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TEDTalks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized(subject to certain rules and regulations).
